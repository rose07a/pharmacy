Opioid Crisis

The United States accounts for 5% of the worlds population but consumes almost 70% of the total global opioid supply, creating an epidemic that has resulted in tens of thousands of deaths each year. How did we get here, and what can we do about it? In this personal talk, Travis Rieder recounts the painful, often-hidden struggle of opioid withdrawal and reveals how doctors quick to prescribe (and over prescribe) opioids aren’t equipped with the tools to eventually get people off the meds. Travis reader, PhD, is the Assistant Director of Education Initiatives, Director of the Master of Bioethics degree program and Research Scholar at the Berman Institute of Bioethics.
